In recent years, people have become increasingly concerned about their health due to the globally spread of emerging infectious diseases. We provide year-round health management services, with a particular focus on the prevention and treatment of lifestyle-related diseases, to help people improve their health.

What is Total Medi

We provide year-round health management services, focusing on lifestyle-related diseases, which include medical checkups in Japan (including sightseeing in Tokyo), follow-up examinations in the client's country, year-round health monitoring, and second opinion services.

OUR SERVICES

Health checkup in Japan (+ sightseeing)

Diabetes, hypertension, dyslipidemia, obesity, heart disease, and stroke are called lifestyle-related diseases. If diabetes, hypertension, and dyslipidemia are left untreated, arteriosclerosis will progress rapidly, and there is a risk of developing cardiovascular disease and other serious complications. In order for our members to lead healthy and youthful lives, we will support our members in receiving high quality medical checkups that focus on lifestyle-related diseases in Tokyo.

Follow-up examination in the
customer's country

After returning to the home country, the follow-up examination is conducted by sending the specimens to Japan and testing them in a Japanese laboratory. From medical examination to follow-up, everything is of Japanese quality.

Year-round monitoring of your health

In order to prevent or control lifestyle-related diseases, annual medical checkups in Japan are not enough.
In this service, we will monitor the test results of our members, manage their health throughout the year, and provide health advice as needed so that they can lead a healthy life.

If you want to see a physician/ need prescription medicine or need hospital referral

You can receive consultations with doctors from Cambodia, Thailand, and Singapore via your mobile phone.
If you need prescription drugs, they will be delivered to your home from Cambodia, Thailand, or Singapore.
Referrals to a Japan/Thailand/Singapore/Cambodia hospital for the best treatment based on your request.
